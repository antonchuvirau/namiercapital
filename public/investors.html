Who we are

Digital first London-based advisory for growth companies.

We help companies to accelerate growth and prepare for commercialisation by providing corporate finance services and attracting strategic investors.

We apply an internally developed matrix and a scorecard to de-risk our portfolio; our network of industrial advisors provides an additional selection filter to choose best-in-class opportunities.

We source investment opportunities from the leading research institutes and universities across the UK and Europe, e.g. Turing Institute, Pasteur Institute, Imperial College London, University of Oxford and others.
