About

Mark was the Co-Founder and Executive Chairman of Lumi Technologies a global market leader in the provision of insight and engagement through mobile devices. Lumi was selected in 2013 in the first cohort of pathfinder UK tech growth companies under the Government’s Future 50 programme.

In April 2017, he effected the disposal of a substantial part of this business to Private Equity Group Vespa Capital.

Over his career to date Mark has worked on 42 IPOs and significant secondary capital raisings in a variety of markets.

Mark joined Morgan Stanley as a Financial Analyst in 1986 and was successively Head of Media Research at SG Warburg, Deutsche Bank, JP Morgan and Dresdner Kleinwort. He was rated the top European Media Analyst for nine years by Institutnal Investor and Reuters surveys.

Highlights

  • Career in Morgan Stanley, SG Warburg, Deutsche Bank, JP Morgan and Dresdner Kleinwort.
  • Seasoned entrepreneur.
  • Co-founder of Lumi Technologies.
  • Selected as a pathfinder UK tech growth company in the initial cohort of Government’s Future 50 programme, along with Just Eat and Zoopla.
