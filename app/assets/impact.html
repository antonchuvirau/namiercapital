Creating impact through our portfolio

We work with companies to help them grow in a sustainable way and make a positive impact.

How we create impact

Environmental value

Namier nurtures businesses that have a positive social or environmental impact.

In the past two years, we have advised and invested in companies in healthcare and environmental tech, that directly benefit the environment and people’s lives.

Long term alignment

We believe that respecting these principles is no longer a nice-to-have option but a critical component of long-term value creation.

Our aim is to provide advice and insight, drawing on the expertise of our extensive network of industrial advisors, that smoothes the path from fundraising through growth to the ultimate crystallisation of value.
